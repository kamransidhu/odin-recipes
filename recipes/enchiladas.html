Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • Tortillas - 2
  • Chicken thigh - 200g
  • Bell Peppers
  • Jalapenos
  • Cheese - amount to preference
  • Greek yoghurt - amount to preference
  • Corriander - One handful
  • Half of a white onion
  • Chicken stock - 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Season chicken with the spice rub of your choice (I chose garlic, salt, oregano and paprika) and throw it in the oven at 225 for 20-25.
  2. Cut bell peppers in half, and put on a baking tray covering the peppers and the tray in a drizzle of oil.
  3. Once the peppers have slightly blackened, put them in a bowl and cover the top, leave for 10 minutes.
  4. After 10 minutes, peel the skin for the bell peppers and throw the skin away.
  5. To a blender, add; Half of the onion, corriander, stock, jalapenos and the roasted peppers.
  6. Once blended, taste and adjust as needed, (I added too much corriander so I can add water to dilute and/or add lemon juice to offset flavour).
  7. The chicken should have been cooked by now, so take out and prepare your tortilla; Add yoghurt to a third of the tortilla, then add chicken and cheese.
  8. Fold the tortilla and add to a baking tray deep enough to hold the sauce we are going to bake the enchiladas in.
  9. As mentioned, place the wrapped tortilla in the tray and cover in the sauce you have just made, cover the enchiladas with cheese if you want.
  10. Bake for up to 10 mins, making sure the cheese has not burned.
  11. Take out, and enjoy!
