Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choosing - 100mg
  • Chicken breast or thigh - 200mg
  • Oil - 5g
  • Garlic - 2 tsp
  • Mozarella
  • Roasted/grilled peppers
  • Tomato puree - 2 tbsp
  • Peas
  • Brocolli
  • Basil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Season chicken with spices of your preference or used left over chicken. I used salt, italian hearbs, chilli flakes and garlic powder. Then throw it in the oven at 225 for 25 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in the pot, once ready, add the garlic and stir for 30 seconds.
  3. Cook the brocolli and peas in the garlic and oil for a few minutes, leave a bit of a bite.
  4. Remove vegetables.
  5. Add the pasta, covering it with boiling water and give a pinch of salt.
  6. Cook the pasta, stirring constantly until the water has been absorbed.
  7. Once cooked down, add tomato puree and stir in.
  8. Add vegetable (including the grilled peppers) and mozarella.
  9. Eat food.
